Bruno Magalhaes

I'm a PhD Neuroscience candidate at the EPFL in Lausanne, Switzerland. I create large scale computer algorithms to map and simulate the neural networks in the brain. My fields of specialization are Parallel and Distributed Computing, Simulation, Machine Learning and Data Science. Prior to this, I graduated with a MSc Advanced Computing from the Imperial College London, with distinction for my research project, and I worked in London, Sao Paulo, New York and Slovenia.
